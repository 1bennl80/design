Instagram Logos

I was asked by a friend to create some basic typography based logos for a number of different instagram accounts that he wanted to start.
The accounts were all centered around sneaker culture so I began by asking my friend which brands he was going to be focusing on with the different accounts, from there I was able to research each brand and get an understand of the different styles they have.
The final design for each of the logos were influenced by the corresponding brand to piggyback on the already established brand recognition.
